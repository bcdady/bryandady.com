# To Do: Bonneville Salt Flats

A visit to the Bonneville Salt Flats in Utah has been on my bucket list for years. The vast expanse of white salt creates an otherworldly landscape that's both serene and spectacular.

<!-- truncate -->

The Bonneville Salt Flats are famous for land speed records and have been featured in countless movies and commercials. I'm particularly interested in visiting during Speed Week when racers from around the world come to test their vehicles on the 10-mile-long racing surface.

Planning to make this trip happen in 2025!
